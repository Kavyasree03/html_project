ASSAM TRAVEL GUIDE


If the seven states of India’s northeast (NE) are fondly called Seven Sisters, Assam has to be the eldest of these. The reason is simple and one has to go back in time to understand why. Four of today’s independent states―Arunachal Pradesh, Meghalaya, Nagaland and Mizoram were part of the undivided Assam till 1972. Present day Assam continues to be the gateway to the North East, thanks to the international airport and the main railway station in Guwahati that serves this entire region. A land of unparalleled natural beauty and mystic aura, Assam is a place where you go to get in touch with your own unexplored self!
